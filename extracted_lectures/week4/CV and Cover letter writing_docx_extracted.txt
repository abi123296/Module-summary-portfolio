Cover letters
What is a cover letter?
A cover letter is an accompanying letter that serves as the introduction to your resume. No resume should be sent without one. The cover letter is individualized for each position for which you are applying. It is an extension of your resume and reflects your knowledge of the employer’s needs. (source:http://www.uweccareerservices.org/resume_guide/documents/Cover%20Letters.pdf)
