Und e r sta nd i ng BAT NA • BATNA refers to the Best Alternative to a Negotiated Agreement and represents the fallback option if negotiations fail. • BATNA is the course of action you pursue if a deal falls through. • Rather than an escape plan, it’s a strategic anchor. • A strong BATNA increases bargaining power and confidence during negotiation.
