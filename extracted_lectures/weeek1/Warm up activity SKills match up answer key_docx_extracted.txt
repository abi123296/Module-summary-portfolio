Activity: Skills Match-Up
Instructions:
Prepare cards with various job roles (Job Role Cards) and cards with corresponding workplace skills (Skill Cards).
Print and cut out enough cards for each student or group of students.
Distribute the cards randomly to the students.
Instruct students to find their skill match based on their job role and stand next to each other once they have found their match.
Facilitate a class discussion to discuss why certain skills are necessary for specific job roles.
Answer Key:
Manager: Leadership (1)
Sales Representative: Communication (2)
Customer Service Agent: Communication (2)
Project Coordinator: Time Management (4)
Marketing Specialist: Creativity (9)
Human Resources Officer: Communication (2)
IT Support Technician: Technical Knowledge (10)
Financial Analyst: Problem-Solving (3)
Event Planner: Adaptability (5)
Administrative Assistant: Time Management (4)
Discussion Points:
Why is communication essential for job roles like Sales Representative and Customer Service Agent?
What skills are needed for effective leadership in a managerial position?
How does problem-solving contribute to the role of a Financial Analyst?
Why is adaptability crucial for an Event Planner?
Discuss the importance of teamwork and time management in different job roles.
